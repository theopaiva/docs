---

Spot trading is the direct exchange of one crypto asset for another at the current market price. This page shows the flow for both order types.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Market Order">
    A market order fills immediately at the best available price.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the trade panel">Select the market pair you want to trade.</Step>
      <Step title="Choose Buy or Sell">Pick your side and enter the amount.</Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">Review the estimated fill and submit. The order executes right away.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Limit Order">
    A limit order fills only at your chosen price or better.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Set your price">Enter the limit price you're willing to accept.</Step>
      <Step title="Enter amount">Specify the quantity to buy or sell.</Step>
      <Step title="Submit">The order rests in the book until matched or cancelled.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
Estimated fills for market orders are not guaranteed. In fast-moving markets, slippage may cause your average price to differ from the quote.
</Warning>

## Cancelling an Order

Open orders can be cancelled any time before they fill from the **Open Orders** panel.

## Related

<Accordion title="What's the difference between In Orders and available balance?">
Funds committed to a resting limit order show under **In Orders** and aren't available for other trades until the order fills or is cancelled.
</Accordion>
